Prefix
Once Upon A Time there was a small school called Hill-side High School in a small, almost invisible town called Turk. In this town, a family of five suffered a terrible loss. A mother, a wife. Her name was Jayden. Jayden was a fine woman; one that every town woman envied. She was smart, kind, and beautiful. But she also had her secrets. No one knew Jayden; not really. She had one particularly dark secret that not even her family knew about. It was this secret that took Jayden from her beloved family.

Jayden was not from this land we call Earth. Well...she was but not from this dimension. On her 14th birthday, little Jayden found a portal to a new land, a new time and a new home. This portal never completely closed. She kept it with her at all times as a reminder of her real home.

Her whole life, Jayden never really truly fit in. She always felt that pull to go back to her own dimension. But one day, this new land she ran off to as a girl, showed he a path to happiness. A path her homeland would never have been able to give her.

"Forgive me, my lady, for being so blunt. But your smile truely lights up this entire room." the strange mn bowed. Jayden stiffled a blushed laugh as she bowed back to the man, "My name is Steven, what shall I call you, miss?"

"You may call me Jay," her smile grew as her cheeks became rosey again, "Short for Jayden."

Jayden and Steven fell in love and had such plans for a large family. But Jayden had not revealed her secret to him. She was afraid he would leave her and tell everyone she was crazy. After having their second daughter, and expecting twin boys,Jayden feared it was too late to tell the truth. He would leave and probably take the kids with him. The truth was, she had been lying to him from the start. She was living a lie. No one she cared about, no one in this realm, knew where she really cam from.
